Hey Retrievers!

Mark your calendars because LUG and SAD are teaming up to have a **SWAP Meet!**

Got old electronics collecting dust? Bring them out! Trade, sell, or give away your old computers, components, cables, gadgets, etc. If its electronic, it's fair game. Give your old hardware a new home! 

Don't have hardware to bring? No worries, is the perfect opportunity to get a hold of some. Been wanting to start a homelab or try out Linux to revive an older device? This is your chance!

- **April 3rd 4:30 PM - 8:00 PM  PAHB 234** (Show up anytime)
- Free pizza and snacks!

This is an open event, so spread the word.
