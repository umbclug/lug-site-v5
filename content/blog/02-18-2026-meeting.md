Tonight's presentation is on the "The Past, Present, and Future of FOSS" by @Penguin

Beyond40 is a grassroots volunteer-network dedicated to bridging the gap from FOSS enthusiasm to FOSS activism. We take a different approach from other groups in the space, focusing on finding creative ways to directly support the technological and social infrastructure that uphold the space. We have multiple in-person chapters across the US dedicated to discussing FOSS issues, educating people, and organizing local community members to use their social and technical skills to contribute to the fight against Big Tech.
