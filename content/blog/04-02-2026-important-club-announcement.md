Due to recent infrastructure instability and after careful review, the UMBC Linux Users Group will be undergoing an immediate rebrand and systems overhaul.

Effective immediately, we are now the:
**☭ Red Star Users Group (RSUG) ☭**

In alignment with this change, all club systems will be migrated to a secure, centrally managed environment based on Red Star OS; a carefully curated Linux distribution designed with security, control, and consistency in mind.

This decision comes after yesterday’s infrastructure outage, which has been formally reclassified as:
> “an unsanctioned demonstration of decentralized system failure”

Key changes include:

- Standardized desktop environment (no customization necessary or permitted)
- Cryptographically signed wallpapers (tampering will be noticed)
- Replacement of “sudo” with a more community-oriented approval process
- External internet access will be limited to the Kwangmyong intranet
- Approved resources will be mirrored internally
- Independent system configuration is discouraged for your convenience

**Next Week’s Meeting:**
Join us for a deep dive into Red Star OS, including its architecture, security model, and what we can learn from highly controlled computing environments.
